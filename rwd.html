Specialties of Pune

Rich Historical Heritage

Pune, known as the "Oxford of the East," has a deep historical significance with landmarks such as Shaniwar Wada, Aga Khan Palace, and Sinhagad Fort.

Vibrant Culture

Pune is a hub of Marathi culture, with festivals like Ganesh Chaturthi celebrated with grandeur. The city also thrives in music, theater, and literature.

Delicious Cuisine

Pune is famous for its food, including Misal Pav, Pithla Bhakri, and Mastani, a rich milkshake dessert.

Famous Places

The city boasts famous spots like Osho Ashram, Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati Temple, and the scenic hills of Lonavala nearby.
